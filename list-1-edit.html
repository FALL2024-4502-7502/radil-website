7 Rom Coms Everyone Should Watch At Least Once In Their Lives

Cliché, predictable, but nevertheless charming and entertaining. That’s the best way to describe the classic romantic comedy. Although which romantic comedy is the best has been heavily debated for as long as the genre has been popular, this non-exhaustive list narrows it down to the ones that are so good, everyone should experience the enjoyment that comes with watching them at some point in their life.

How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days (2003)

Directed by Donald Petrie

How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days is a romantic comedy about two people with clashing plans. Andie is trying to drive a guy away for an article, while Ben is trying to make her fall for him to win a bet. Their hilarious antics lead to unexpected romance. It’s a fun, feel-good movie that’s easy to love.

Crazy Rich Asians (2018)

Directed by Jon M. Chu

Crazy Rich Asians is a romantic comedy about Rachel Chu, who visits Singapore with her boyfriend and learns he’s from a very wealthy family. As she faces family drama and challenges to her relationship, Rachel’s world changes completely. The film is full of humor, charm, and beautiful scenes. It’s a fun, heartfelt story that’s perfect for rom-com lovers.

10 Things I Hate About You (1999)

Directed by Gil Junger

10 Things I Hate About You is a fun teen comedy inspired by Shakespeare's The Taming of the Shrew. It follows the outspoken Kat Stratford as she falls for the charming Patrick Verona. With great humor and heartfelt moments, it’s a movie people love to watch again and again. Its mix of romance and individuality makes it a timeless favorite.

Clueless (1995)

Directed by Amy Heckerling

Clueless is a fun teen comedy about Cher, a fashionable high school student in Beverly Hills. She helps her friends with love and life while figuring out her own relationships. With its funny moments and iconic characters, the film has become a classic. It’s an entertaining movie that still connects with audiences today.

When Harry Met Sally

Directed by Rob Reiner

When Harry Met Sally is a classic romantic comedy about two friends who wonder if men and women can just be friends. As they meet over the years, they experience love, heartbreak, and friendship. With unforgettable lines and a heartwarming story, it’s a favorite rom-com.

Crazy, Stupid, Love (2011)

Directed by Glenn Ficarra & John Requa

Crazy, Stupid, Love is a romantic comedy about Cal, who gets help from a bachelor named Jacob after his divorce. As Cal tries dating again, surprising twists and love stories happen. With a great cast and funny, heartfelt moments, the film shows love in many ways. It’s a fun, feel-good movie that will make you laugh and smile.

Notting Hill (1999)

Directed by Roger Michell

Notting Hill is a romantic comedy about William, a bookstore owner, who falls in love with a famous actress named Anna. As they try to make their relationship work, they face many challenges. With a sweet story and memorable characters, it’s a timeless love story. Notting Hill continues to be a favorite for its warmth and charm.
